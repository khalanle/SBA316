Strawberry Basil Gin Smash

  • Bombay Sapphire gin
  • Fresh lime juice
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Chopped strawberries
  • Simple syrup
  • Tonic water

  1. Add strawberries, lime juice, and basil leaves to a glass with 1 oz of simple syrup and muddle ingredients.
  2. Measure and pour 2 oz of gin to the glass and fill with ice.
  3. Fill glass with tonic water and stir.
  4. Garnish with lime slice and enjoy!
